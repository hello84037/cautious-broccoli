History

Nestled in what used to be Lake Boneville, Davis County is a lush valley with beautiful sites, tourism and people. Inhabited by Native Americans originally, then later settled by pioneers of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints, this beautiful valley offers many businesses, community events, and a family friendly environment we love.
