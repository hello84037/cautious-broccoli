Our Mission

As the Davis County Chamber of Commerce, we work to pursue exellence in business, community involvement, and human kindness. We strive to work to bring our community together in a way that lifts each of us individually, as well as helps us to rise collectively. We are striving to build a better tomorrow.

Our Goals

Leadership

We are leaders in our community, exemplifying cooperation, direction, and encouragement to those we serve.

Collaboration

We work to actively listen and foster trust among members of our community.

Progress

We work to raise all in the community higher together. We work with members of the community to improve and become stronger together.

Community

We include all members of our community, young and old, new or life-long citizen. We believe we all have something to offer/
